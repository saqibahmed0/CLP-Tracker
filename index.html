Scope & disclaimer. This tool implements the CLP calculation/summation methods for health & environmental hazards (ATEmix additivity; generic concentration limits for skin/eye/STOT; M-factor summation for aquatic). Physical hazards (flammable, oxidising, corrosive-to-metals) cannot be derived from composition alone and are flagged for testing. New hazard classes (ED, PBT/vPvB, PMT/vPvM) from Regulation (EU) 2023/707 are included with mandatory mixture thresholds (May 2026). SDS import and web lookup extract H-codes automatically — always review against the source SDS / ECHA C&L Inventory. Not a substitute for a qualified safety assessor.
